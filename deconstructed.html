Deconstructed: a poem book about design

This project is typography based publication called Deconstructed. Each spread has two famous quotes about design or typography. Then, there is a quote that is made of words from each of the two famous quotes. In this book, I tried to add lots of physical qualities to make it look more hand crafted. There are torn pieces of paper textures, crumpled pieces of paper textures, and hand-drawn words. I used stencils to make the hand-drawn words so they were the same font. This book uses hierarchy to emphasize more important words in the quotes from each other. This is created by using different fonts, sizes, and typefaces. I tried to make every page more exciting and unique so that a reader would not get bored with the repetitive nature in the structure of the book.
