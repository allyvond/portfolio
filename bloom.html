Part 1: Creating the Brand

Bloom is a mock skincare brand I made to show off my design and branding skills. This brand is inspired by most of the skincare products I use because I have incredibly dry and sensitive skin. The design inspiration for this brand actually came from hydrangeas. They are my favorite flower and their color tones are so pretty. The shapes that define the brand are based off of abstracted petals. I made them abstract because I was going for a very modern branding style.

Part 3: Creating the Motion Graphic

For this motion graphic, I made an advertisment for Bloom. I used free videos and auto for this project, but the mockups and shapes involved are mine. This ad was heavily inspired by modern skincare and makeup brands such as Glossier and Drunk Elephant.The branding for this ad like the skincare itself had heavy white space. It included floating shapes that are patterns of the brand to tie in the feel of the brand.
